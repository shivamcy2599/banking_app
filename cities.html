Delhi

Delhi, city and national capital territory, north-central India. The city of Delhi actually consists of two components: Old Delhi, in the north, the historic city; and New Delhi, in the south, since 1947 the capital of India, built in the first part of the 20th century as the capital of British India.

Noida

Noida is in the Gautam Budh Nagar district of Uttar Pradesh. It is a part of the national capital region and is just 14 km from Connaught Place which is considered as the heart of the capital. The administrative headquarters though are situated in Greater Noida. The city is connected to Maharani Bagh locality in Delhi via the 550 meters long, eight lane DND Toll which is built over Yamuna river.
